    While there are many Disney Channel stars that are continuing to ride on their success, some are just not appreciated as much as someone like Miley Cyrus or Nick Jonas. One of the most criminally underrated stars from the network is Bridgit Mendler, who appeared as recurring characters in Wizards of Waverly Place and Jonas. Eventually, she would become the star of Good Luck Charlie and became a decent success.

    Not only is she underappreciated as an actress, but Bridgit is also an incredible singer too. You might know her for her song "Ready or Not," which remains her most iconic song to date. So when fans found out that Bridgit announced her musical comeback, they have the craziest, but excited reactions ever.

    Related: 10 Disney Channel Stars Who Are Married

    It has been nearly ten years since Bridgit has released her debut album Hello My Name Is.... So to see fans being exhilarated at the fact that she's returning is exciting. They have been waiting for so long and it will be interesting to see what Bridgit will bring in her new line of work. Also, she is working on her Ph.D while this is occurring, so the amount of work she's putting in has earned Bridgit so much respect from her dedicated fans.

    As Twitter user @TheOnlyMilah puts it, it warmed their heart to see Bridgit trend on the site and being one who puts out actual decent music. With the preview she has put on her official Twitter account, fans already have the song stuck in their heads. To their credit, it is insanely catchy.

    Related: Wizards Of Waverly Place: Where Is The Cast Now?

    Fans also pointed out that Bridgit announced her comeback before Lorde and Rihanna of all people, which is saying a lot given how much time both artists like to take off before coming back with new music. As fans celebrate, they compared Bridgit coming back with new music as if the pandemic is ending or the Avatar from Avatar: The Last Airbender returning after so long. This year has turned for the better for her fans and now all they have to do is wait and listen in for new song teasers or possible music videos.

    2021 looks to be a promising year for someone like Bridgit. Hopefully she will make a strong music comeback and win over critics and new fans alike.
