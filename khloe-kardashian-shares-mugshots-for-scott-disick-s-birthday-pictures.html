    12 Sep 2024, 00:00

    All hail, Lord Disick! Scott Disick may have spent his holiday weekend in Europe for the epic extravaganza that was Kim Kardashian and Kanye West‘s wedding, but that didn’t stop the reality TV family from celebrating his birthday on Monday, May 26. 

    The entrepreneur and businessman turned 31, and Khloe and Rob Kardashian took to social media to congratulate him. 

    “Happy birthday to my partner in crime @letthelordbewithyou I love you LD!!” Khloe captioned a split photo of her mugshot and Disick’s.

    Kardashian was arrested in 2007 for a DUI and spent only three hours in jail due to overcrowding. Disick’s mugshot comes from his 2001 drunk driving arrest after he crashed his car in Riverhead, New York.

    The mugshots weren’t the only comical tribute to Disick from the Kardashian family. Pal Rob Kardashian also tweeted a photo of him struggling to carry Kourtney Kardashian‘s longtime boyfriend in a rickshaw-like cart through the ocean while on vacation.
