    Ashton Kutcher just revealed that his hairline is slowly receding during an appearance on Conan. Luckily, Kutcher has a plan for when his hair finally bites the dust.

    Instead of going with the bald look, Kutcher plans on rocking a baseball hat to hide his receding locks. During his talk with Conan O’Brien, the actor admitted that his hair has been receding since he was in his mid-20s.

    He took medication to keep his hair from continuing its rearward march, but he stopped the meds when he started having kids.

    According to Entertainment Tonight, Kutcher plans on just letting his hair fall out naturally and wear hats to hide it from the public. “I'm a baseball cap guy, so I think I'm OK,” he explained in the interview, adding that nobody will know because he will “have the ball cap on.” He will not, however, wear a toupee, no matter how bad his hairline gets.

    Kutcher and his wife, Mila Kunis, originally met on the set of That ‘70s Show. The couple shares two children together: Wyatt and Dimitri. While the pair continues to grow their family, Kunis was recently asked about potentially reviving the hit series that brought them together.

    Kunis revealed that no reboot plans were in the works but that she is open to the idea. She also explained how everyone from the show is still good friends and that it wouldn’t take much to get them all together.

    While Kutcher’s hairline is failing him, the actor is making a big difference in the real world. Kutcher’s organization, Thorn: Digital Defenders of Children, has been making great strides in the battle against human trafficking.

    In a recent report, the organization revealed that it helped identify close to 6,000 child sex victims and rescued over 100 kids from sexual abuse in 2017. Kutcher’s former wife, Demi Moore, co-founded the organization back in 2009.

    Kutcher and Moore’s organization uses technology to help save children from human trafficking. Last year, the actor spoke about the organization in front of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee to get more support in the fight to save kids from sexual abuse.

    Ashton Kutcher is currently starring in the Netflix series The Ranch, which is gearing up for the premiere of its third season on the streaming service.
