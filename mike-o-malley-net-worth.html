    Mike O’Malley is an American actor and writer who has a net worth of $15 million.

    In 1991, O’Malley became the charismatic host of the Nickelodeon children’s game show Get the Picture. A year later, his energetic hosting style earned him another gig as the host of Nickelodeon Guts, later evolving into Global GUTS from 1992 to 1995. These shows catapulted O’Malley into the limelight, setting the stage for his move to Los Angeles to pursue an acting career, a decision influenced by the advice of fellow television personality Marc Summers.

    Beyond acting, O’Malley delved into playwriting, with notable works such as Three Years from Thirty and Diverting Devotion, both published and produced by Off-Broadway. His creative talents extended to screenwriting, evidenced by his work on the film Certainty, based on his play Searching for Certainty.

    Currently, O’Malley continues to captivate viewers as a regular on the TNT series Snowpiercer, a testament to his enduring relevance in the television landscape.
