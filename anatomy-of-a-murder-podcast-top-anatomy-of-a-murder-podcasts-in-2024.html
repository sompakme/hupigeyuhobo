From unsolved mysteries to high-profile crimes, Crime Junkie caters to true crime enthusiasts seeking compelling narratives.

    Crimetown

    “Crimetown” is a critically acclaimed podcast that explores the criminal underworld and political corruption in various American cities. Created by Zac Stuart-Pontier and Marc Smerling, each season focuses on a different city and its unique criminal history. By uncovering untold stories and interviewing key players, “Crimetown” provides listeners with a captivating glimpse into the dark underbelly of urban crime.

    Up and Vanished

    Hosted by Payne Lindsey, “Up and Vanished” delves into cold cases and missing person mysteries, aiming to shed light on unresolved crimes. Payne’s relentless investigation and interviews with those connected to the cases create a sense of urgency and intrigue. With its compelling storytelling and dedication to uncovering the truth, “Up and Vanished” keeps listeners on the edge of their seats.

    Someone Knows Something

    “Someone Knows Something,” hosted by award-winning filmmaker David Ridgen, is a podcast that dives deep into unsolved mysteries. David’s meticulous research and compassionate interviews offer a fresh perspective on cases that have puzzled investigators and left families searching for answers. Through his storytelling, David invites listeners to join him on a quest for truth and justice.

    CounterClock

Using in-depth research and firsthand interviews, Delia unravels complex cases, revealing shocking details and shedding light on the criminal justice system. With its engaging storytelling and meticulous investigation, “CounterClock” captivates audiences seeking a deeper understanding of perplexing crimes.
