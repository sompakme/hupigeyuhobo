    Ana De Armas' Age, Height, Relationship Status &amp; Other Cool Facts About The New Bond Girl

    12 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Ana de Armas has recently been making waves on the big screen, and while she continues to star in blockbuster films, it's time for fans to really get to know who this actress is. She's the newest Bond girl, starring alongside Daniel Craig, in No Time To Die.

    RELATED: Everything We Know About The Upcoming James Bond Film, No Time To Die

    However, this definitely isn't her first time being in a popular flick. Before she's set to hit the big screen again for this highly-anticipated action movie, fans need to learn everything there is to know about this actress, from her professional career to her personal life.

    10 She Was Born In 1988

    Ana de Armas was born on April 30th, 1988, meaning she just turned 32 in 2020. She isn't a fresh face in Hollywood, but she's still young enough that she has so much time to offer fans many more incredible performances.

    This Taurus is as lovable and grounded as anyone in this sign, and also hardworking and intuitive. Ana de Armas has certainly proved she's these things.

    9 She's 5-foot-6

    For those that are already huge fans of Ana de Armas, it might be kind of surprising that she's a little above average height. 5-foot-6 is certainly not short for a woman, and she doesn't need any special shoes to match her co-stars.

    In fact, Daniel Craig is only 4 inches taller than her! Even her previous co-stars, like Chris Evans and Ryan Gosling, had a six-inch lead on her, so directors may be happy that this pair are closer in height.

    8 She's A Cuban Actress

    Before Ana de Armas made it big in the United States, she had her fair share of acting in Spanish films and television. In fact, she still dabbles in international film, keeping close to her roots.

    RELATED: 10 Facts To Know About The Young & Upcoming Florence Pugh

    She was born and raised in Cuba, but later moved to Spain, where her acting career took off. There are some great English AND Spanish films to binge that star this talented gal.

    7 She's Tied To Ben Affleck

    It wasn't until about March 2020 that fans could decisively say that these two popular actors were romantically involved. From going to Cuba together and quarantining together to running errands, they're pretty darn adorable.

    While there's a bit of an age gap between these two, this Batman actor and Cuban actress are proving that love is in the air! She's even been able to hang out with his kids.

    6 She's Been Married Before

    For those who are still not convinced that Ben Affleck and Ana de Armas can make it last, it's probably important to consider that they have a lot in common when it comes to real life.

    RELATED: Things Ben Affleck Has Done Since Divorcing Jennifer Garner

    Ana de Armas was married from 2011 to 2013 to Marc Clotet. Of course, Affleck was married to Jennifer Garner for 13 years, before they split in 2018. These two prefer to be alone, but are finding some happiness together - which makes them a perfect pair.

    5 She Moved To Los Angeles In 2014

    Ana de Armas moved to Los Angeles after having a successful career in Spain for years, but when she moved, she wasn't great at speaking English. Still, she persevered, and a year later starred in Knock Knock with Keanu Reeves.

    There's a lot of reasons to love this woman, including her talent and quirky personality, but she also worked incredibly hard to get to where she is - and deserves so much respect for it.

    4 She Was First A 'Blade Runner Girl'

    By the time Blade Runner 2049 hit the big screen in 2017, Ana de Armas had already had prominent roles in War Dogs, Knock Knock, and Overdrive. However, nothing was quite as big as this revamped sequel.

    With Ryan Gosling, Ana de Armas played Joi, which was essentially the equivalent of a Bond girl in the Blade Runner universe. Of course, she's much more than a pretty face and slayed the memorable role in this Oscar-winning flick.

    3 She Has An Adorable Maltese

    If there's one thing all fans like to learn about their favorite celebrities, it's about their pets. Ana de Armas has a super cute white Maltese named Elvis.

    RELATED: 10 Facts We Just Found Out About Oscar-Winner Charlize Theron

    For those who aren't already following this lovable and adorable actress on Instagram, it's time to do so. Followers will get inside looks at her career, stunning photoshoots, and most importantly, pictures of Elvis.

    2 She Has A Golden Globe Nomination

    Ana de Armas had her first real starring role in Hollywood in 2019, when she played Marta in the Rian Johnson-directed flick, Knives Out. She also starred with Daniel Craig in this film, along with Jamie Lee Curtis and Chris Evans.

    If fans recognize this stunning Bond girl but can't place her, it might just be from this award-winning, hilarious, and entertaining 'whodunnit' flick.

    1 She'll Be Playing Marilyn Monroe

    Everyone that knows anything about this actress knows that her next film role could be her defining moment as an actress. Andrew Dominik is directing Blonde, a chronicle of Marilyn Monroe's life.

    After just one audition, Ana de Armas proved that she could transform into a blonde and bring this iconic figure to the big screen. Set to premiere in 2021, fans should definitely be excited.
