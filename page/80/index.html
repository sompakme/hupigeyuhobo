Birdman And Slim To Be Honored At YouTubes Leaders And Legends Gala

25 Aug 2024, 00:00

Bryan “Birdman” Williams and Ronald “Slim” Williams, founders of Cash Money Records, are set to be honored for their accomplishments in the music industry in alignment with Black Music Month. YouTube announced the two Hip-Hop moguls as the honorees for its 2024 Leaders and Legends Gala. Set for June 27 in Los Angeles, the invite-only affair is YouTube’s way of celebrating entrepreneurial achievements and groundbreaking founders in black music and culture. The evening will consist of a star-studded lineup of performances, and Lyor Cohen, Global Head of Music at YouTube, presenting Birdman and Slim with the 2024 Leaders and Legends Award.
