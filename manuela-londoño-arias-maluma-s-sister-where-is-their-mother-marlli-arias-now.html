    Columbian singer Maluma was born to Luis Londono and Marlli Arias and grew up with his sister, Manuela Londona Arias.

    The singer’s real name is Juan Luis Londono Arias. Born and brought up in Medellin, Maluma developed an interest in music at a relatively young age. He urban recording songs at the age of sixteen.

    In 2012, the artist released his debut album; however, his breakthrough album was 2015’s Pretty Boy, Dirty Boy. The album led to notable collaborations with several famous artists. In 2018, he released F.A.M.E, to further commercial success.

    Maluma’s hit single “Hawai” ranked number three on the Billboard Global 200 and further became the first number one single to be listed on the Billboard Global Excl. U.S. chart.

    The talented singer grew up with his big sister, Manuela, and both share a perfect relationship. However, only a few particulars about his singer are known. So, here is everything we know about the famed sister of the singer.

    Meet Maluma Older Sister Manuela Londono Arias

    Manuela Londono Arias is the lovely sister of Colombian singer, songwriter, and actor Maluma.

    They have a perfect relationship and are often spotted at various events and occasions. The singer often dedicates pictures and phrases to his dear sister. Manuela also does the same and shares beautiful images of herself with her brother on social accounts.

    Manuela has supported and encouraged her brother since the beginning of his career, to the point that she presides over the organization named ‘El Arte de los Suenos.” She has dedicated herself to living in her hometown.

    On October 2, 2021, she shared a joyous picture of herself with Maluma and captioned it, “Thank you, parents, you didn’t just give me a younger brother, but my best friend also.” The sweet post clearly defines how close the siblings are.

    Unlike Maluma, his sister has directed her life towards academics. She has lived all her life in Medellin and studied Psychology as her first career. Later, Manuela delved a little into Philosophy studies.

    Despite not being so media, Manuela has been notable for several successful projects. She is quite famous on Instagram under the username @m_londo. With 1,795 posts, she enjoys a fan base of over 368k followers.

    Where Is Maluma’s Mother Marlli Arias Now?

    Singer Maluma’s loving mother Marlii Arias is currently leading her life out of the spotlight. The famous mother of the Columbian artist is a successful businesswoman.

    Marlli is a proud mother of two kids, a daughter, and a son with her beloved husband Luis Fernando Londono. Born in 1969 in Medellin, she is fifty-three years old and holds Columbian nationality.

    She still looks stunning even in her fifties. Marlli married her husband in the early 1990s and have been together for over three decades. She shares a beautiful bonding with her kids.

    Maluma often mentions his beautiful mother as the most important person in his life. His mother frequently travels with him during his concerts and world tour. She is also active on Instagram under the nametag @mar_ariasg.

    Her Instagram has pictures of her son and family shared among 332k followers. Marlli enjoys a huge fan following on the platform where she shares cute moments with her sin during concerts and tours.

    Manuela Londoño Arias And Maluma Age Gap

    Manuela is probably in her early 30s, while her younger brother Maluma is twenty-eight years old. The artist was born on January 28, 1994, in Medellin, Columbia.

    Although Manuela’s exact birth date is not known, she celebrates her birthday on December 19. She studied psychology and philosophy and has been inhabiting Medellin since her birth.

    Moreover, she is a lovely mother to her sons, Romeo and Apolo. Together with Maluma, she launched El rate de los Suenos (The Art of Dreams) foundation. The organization helps children and teenagers to find their way in life via art.

    The musical artist chose the stage name Maluma, as it is a combination of the first syllable of the names of his mom Marlli, his dad Luis, and his older sister Manuela.

    In 2013, the artist received several nominations, including the Best New Artist at the Latin Grammy Awards and the Best Latin America Central Act at the MTV Europe Music Awards. He won the Best radio Song at the Shock Awards for his song La Temperatura.

    In 2017, he received nominations for two categories at the Billboard Music Awards. In 2019, Maluma received EI Premio ASCAP for Songwriter of the year. Later in 2020, the artist received MTV Video Music Award for Best Latin.
