His hard work and training have also proved to be very lucrative. Travis Kelce's salary as a tight end for the Kansas City Chiefs is just as high as fans would expect, and his contract has offered him lots of financial security.

In this article, we'll take a look at Travis Kelce's salary and contract, the property he owns, and the amazing $6 million home he purchased in 2023.

    How Travis Kelce's Salary From The NFL Helped Travis Kelce Buy Several Houses

    According to Chiefs Wire/USA Today, the tight end has been earning millions during his time in the NFL. Travis Kelce's salary in 2023 was$11.25 million. While he might not have as much money as his girlfriend Taylor Swift, who has a billion-dollar net worth, he's more than comfortable, and that has enabled him to spend millions on various properties. Of course, he has still had to adjust to his increased visibility because of his romance with Swift as all the media attention has taken some getting used to.

    As Hello! Magazine reported, Travis Kelce has a few houses thanks to his comfortable financial situation. According to the Robb Report, Kelce bought a huge house for $6 million in Leawood, Kansas City in October 2023. He has another house in Kansas City that isn't quite as big and is said to be valued at $1 million. Kelce purchased the Kansas City house in 2019 for $995,000. Jason and Travis Kelce also bought an Orlando, Florida condo for $355,000 along with Donna Kelce, their mom.

He also bought the house because of his relationship with Taylor Swift. He not only has his own golf course, which would be amazing enough, but he also has a pickleball/tennis court, a pool, and a hot tub. As Hello!
