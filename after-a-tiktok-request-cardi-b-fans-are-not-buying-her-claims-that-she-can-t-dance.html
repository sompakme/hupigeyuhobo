    In her 2018 breakout-hit, "Bodak Yellow," Cardi B tells fans, "Look, I don't dance now, I make money moves." Since then, Cardi B has won a Grammy, charted on Billboard's Hot 100 list, and, most recently, collaborated with Megan Thee Stallion. While their collaboration has garnered criticism from celebrities, including David Spade and Ben Shapiro, their song continues to dominate the charts. With this record's success, fans have been patiently waiting for Cardi B to share a video of her dancing to the song on TikTok. In response, she makes the same claim from "Bodak Yellow," explaining her lack of dancing abilities on Twitter.

    Related: Cardi B Debuts On TikTok With Sexy Dance Challenge On Husband Offset

    While Cardi B might believe that she can't dance, her Twitter followers rushed to the comments to dismiss her claims. Many users cited specific evidence from her online history and past performances to prove that she can dance.

    Aside from Cardi B dancing throughout the WAP video, one Twitter user reminded her that she danced to the song on her Instagram live. Not to mention, other users referred to her history as a stripper before becoming a prominent music artist. If she had danced as a stripper, they believed that she could dance. While most fans referred to specific instances where Cardi displayed her dancing prowess, some made a comprehensive list of her dancing history.

    Related: Best Twitter Reactions To Beyonce's Coachella Performance

    One user talked about her performances from different award shows and concerts. As most fans made different arguments on behalf of Cardi's dancing abilities, some fans simply voiced their frustration and displeasure with her perceived dishonesty.

    In the past few months, a few music artists have made false promises to their fans. The most prominent examples are with Doja Cat and Kanye West. Notoriously, Doja Cat promised fans that if they got her song "Say So" to number 1 on the Billboard Hot 100, she would expose her chest. However, once the song reached #1, she admitted that she lied to them. Kanye West was set to release an album on July 24th, but it never came out. Thus, Cardi B fans can only hope that she doesn't follow in the footsteps of Kanye and Doja, who never fulfilled their promises to fans. Hopefully, fans' Twitter comments inspire her to post her dance on TikTok.
