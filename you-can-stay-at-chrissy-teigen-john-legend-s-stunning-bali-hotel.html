    You Can Stay At Chrissy Teigen &amp; John Legend's Stunning Bali Hotel

    Over the last few weeks, couple Chrissy Teigen and John Legend have been on a luxurious vacation in Bali with daughter Luna, 2; and son Miles, 3 months. With each celebrity posting jaw-dropping photos on their respective Instagrams, it's hard not to feel jealous about their vacation.

    27 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Over the last few weeks, couple Chrissy Teigen and John Legend have been on a luxurious vacation in Bali with daughter Luna, 2; and son Miles, 3 months. With each celebrity posting jaw-dropping photos on their respective Instagrams, it's hard not to feel jealous about their vacation.

    As People recently pointed out, you can stay at the same resort as the couple. The Como Shambhala Estate is located in Ubud, on the upper hills of Bali. The resort doesn't come cheap, though; its rooms and suites start as low as $540 per night. Villas with two or three bedrooms also start at around $1000 a night, and there are also nearby residences with private pools. Needless to say, it's not exactly an inexpensive choice for a place to stay during a trip to Bali.

    RELATED: NEW LAGUNA BEACH HOTEL WILL BE YOUR NEXT CALIFORNIA ESCAPE

    Outside of its fancy appearance and high price tag, this resort is also known for being extremely-health conscious. Guests can expect to see specialized menus and activities such as rafting and cycling. You'll even meet with a doctor or nutritionist who will give you a meal plan based on the Ayurvedic diet to enjoy during your stay. This means that, should you stay at the Como Shambhala Estate, you'll be able to maintain a healthy lifestyle despite being far away from home.

    As for Teigen, Legend and their two kids, the young family seemed to enjoy their stay at the Como Shambhala Estate by relaxing. This included lounging by the pool numerous times while extended family also showed up. The couple documented their trip on their Instagram accounts—from their kids to chilling by the pool.

    If you're thinking of visiting Bali—be it soon or sometime in the distant future—the Como Shambhala Estate is not a cheap option for a place to stay. But its focus on health and well-being, paired with gorgeous rooms and an equally gorgeous backdrop in Ubud, there's a lot to appeal to any and all tourists. If you can or are willing to shell out the extra cash, then this fancy resort is the ideal place to stay during your trip to Bali.
