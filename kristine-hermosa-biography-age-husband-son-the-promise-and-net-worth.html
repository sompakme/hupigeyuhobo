    Kristine Hermosa Biography

    Kristine Hermosa is a Filipina actress who is best known for her roles in Pangako Sa ‘Yo and other famous television series. Her back to back success was known in the 2000 and 2010 multiple soap operas. She was discovered when she was only 12 years old after her older sister Kathleen auditioned for a show in ABS-CBN.

    Her first role was in the weekly 1998 drama series Sa Sandaling Kailangan Mo Ako from there she was given a role in 2000 opera Pangako Sa ‘Yo alongside Jericho Rosales. her lead role became prominent in the short-lived film titled Till Death Do Us Part in 2005. Kristine later appeared in Gulong ng Palad and Prinsesa ng Banyera in 2006.

    This followed a role in Dahil May Isang Ikaw in 2009. Her last project In ABS-CBN was the action-fantasy series Noah where she played a diwata the fairy Counterpart. She then made her official return via GMA’s weekly sitcom Hay, Bahay with her husband Oyo Boy Sotto and other famous actors. in 2018, she made her drama breakthrough by playing a very special role in the adventure-fantasy drama Bagani.
