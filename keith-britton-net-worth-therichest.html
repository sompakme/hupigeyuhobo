    Keith Britton is a businessman who has an estimated net worth of $8 million. He was born on September 23, 1976 in Sandown, New Hampshire, USA, and comes from a family that owns a small business. He graduated from the University of Massachusetts with a degree in finance, and joined the corporate world right after, ultimately finding it ‘unsatisfying’.

    Britton earned his net worth as an actor and model, as well as the co-founder and Chief Executive Officer of My Fashion Database. As an actor, he was featured on Cold Case and As The World Turns Round. He also did a stint in modelling, which was a motivator for his own fashion concept.

    Britton's My Fashion Database began in the summer of 2008. An IMDb-style site that started in his home basement, the site has 70,000 profiles and contacts accessible to fashion industry professionals. Wall Street Journal, Vogue and Fashionista declared the site the best thing to happen to the fashion industry.

    Keith and his ex-fiancé, actress Zoe Saldana, founded the business. After 11 years of being together, they decided to end their relationship in November 2011. They remain as business partners of My Fashion Database.

    Britton is also the founder of Juggernaut Investment, a private angel fund with a focus on digital media, fashion consumer web and technologies, as well as Juggernaut Consulting, and Vizcloud, an image management and distributing service.
