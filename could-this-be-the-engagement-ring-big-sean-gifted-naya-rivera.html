    Could This Be The Engagement Ring Big Sean Gifted Naya Rivera?

    Naya Rivera may be trading in her high school cheerleading outfit for a wedding dress. The Glee starlets rep reportedly confirmed to JustJared that she and rapper Big Sean are engaged after six months of dating.

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Naya Rivera may be trading in her high school cheerleading outfit for a wedding dress. The Glee starlet’s rep reportedly confirmed to JustJared that she and rapper Big Sean are engaged after six months of dating.

    Their relationship sparked from the G.O.O.D. Music MC whispering into Naya’s DM box on Twitter.

    “I had followed him and he sent me a little message like, ‘I’m a fan.’ I tweeted that I followed him, I was trying to get his attention,” she told Access Hollywood. “We went to dinner and the rest is history.”

    At Thursday night’s Latina magazine party, the cover girl sported a flashy rock on her ring finger (above), sparking the rumors.

    Sean recently spoke to VIBE on his thoughts of marriage and having kids, saying that “it all depends.”

    When are you going to have kids of your own?
    Shit. I don’t know… now, I’m about that age. Maybe sooner or later.

    Do you want to get married first?
    Yeah, I guess. It just all depends. In a perfect world, if it could work out like that, then that would be cool… to be married and have kids. If I had a kid I would take care of it and be a good dad. That’s when you really have to boss up. I would probably go even harder if that happened.

    VIBE reached out to both of their reps for confirmation and will update this story as more details come in.
