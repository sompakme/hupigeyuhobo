    Packing their bags! Summer House premiered in 2017 — and not every cast member has had a lasting residence in the Hamptons.

    Season 1, which starred Kyle Cooke, Cristina Gibson, Lindsay Hubbard, Stephen McGee, Carl Radke, Jaclyn Shuman, Everett Weston and twins Ashley and Lauren Wirkus, debuted on Bravo on January 2017.

    The pilot aired as part of an episode of Vanderpump Rules as Stassi Schroeder, Katie Maloney, Scheana Shay and Kristen Doute traveled to the East Coast.

    After Cristina and Jaclyn left the show following the debut season, the cast expanded to include Carl’s ex Danielle Olivera and his friend Amit Neuman. Kyle’s now-wife, Amanda Batula, was also upgraded to a full-time cast member. Everett, for his part, faded into the background after his 2017 split from Lindsay, which played out on season 2 of the show.

    By the time season 3 rolled around, there was another cast shakeup as the Wirkus twins and Stephen departed.

    “Much has been speculated about the Summer House cast over the last few days. Regretfully, we can confirm that we will not be full-time cast members for the third season,” Lauren and Ashley said in a joint statement to Us Weekly in 2019. “We depart Summer House with nothing but good wishes to those at Bravo, and to the cast and crew for helping to create life-lasting memories over the last two years.”

    Paige DeSorbo and Hannah Berner rounded out the cast for season 3, but after three years on the series, Hannah made her exit.
