    Keke Wyatt Announced Divorce From Zackariah Darring After 5 Years

    Keke Wyatt is officially a free woman. After being spotted out with her new rumored beau that sparked fans to question the whereabouts of her husband, Zackariah Darring, the Certified singer revealed that she is divorced.

    31 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Keke Wyatt is officially a free woman.

    After being spotted out with her new rumored beau that sparked fans to question the whereabouts of her husband, Zackariah Darring, the Certified singer revealed that she is divorced.

    “I am divorced and soooo HAPPY to be FREE from that bondage and abuse!!!” Wyatt told The Shade Room. Over the course of their five-year marriage, she and Darring had two children together, Ke’Riah David (4), and Ke’Zyah Jean (2), who was born with the genetic disorder trisomy 13.

    The couple was notoriously private and didn’t publicize any tension, especially on her 2023 reality show, Keke Wyatt’s World. However, the 42-year-old was vocal about how she often cared for Ke’Zyah alone.

    Questions and rumors about her marriage began after she was seen out with a man named Anthony Whitehead. When asked about him, Wyatt replied, “[A] girl can’t just have a friend? Stop with all that. Okay, ‘cause, like, I’m out here just chilling. Can’t just chill, have fun — I’m just having fun, chilling. That part.”

    When asked about Darring directly, she quipped, “Where’s my husband? Well, I don’t have a husband. Boom. How about that?”

    She doubled down on the statement, telling fans on the livestream, “Go get him,” and “Let him choke your a**.”

    This was Wyatt’s third marriage. Her last husband, Michael Jamar Ford, asked for a divorce while she was pregnant and dealing with one of her son’s battle with cancer. He is now in remission.

    The news comes on the heels of Wyatt releasing her long-awaited new album, Certified, which marks her first LP in seven years.
