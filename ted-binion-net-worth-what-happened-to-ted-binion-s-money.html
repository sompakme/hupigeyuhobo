    Ted Binion was an American gambling executive well known as the son of casino magnate Benny Binion. Over the years, he developed a severe addiction to heroin and also was involved in drug trafficking for which he was arrested. He then lost his gaming license which affected him mentally and he went into depression.

    On 17 September 1998, he was mysteriously found dead on the floor of his Las Vegas estate. He was 54 years old. His then-girlfriend Sandy Murphy was first suspected and charged with his murder but was later acquitted.
