    Actress and model Lily Collins is the daughter of famous musician Phil Collins. Despite having a famous father, Lily has carved her name in the entertainment industry on her own. She has starred in some prominent films and even starred alongside Sandra Bullock and Pretty Woman actress Julia Roberts. The talented actress first gained attention as a writer for some popular teen magazines such as Seventeen and Teen Vogue.

    The actress later entered the world of entertainment and has since became of the most recognized actress in Hollywood. Lily Collins has accumulated a net worth of $25 million through the years and is currently leading the series Emily In Paris portraying the titular role.

    Tolkien ($9 Million)

    Tolkien was released in May 10, 2019 and has grossed about $9 million in the worldwide box office. The film directed by Dome Karukoski with the screeplay by David Gleeson, and Stephen Beresford is about the formative years of J.R.R. Tolkien as a young student. The story revolves around the time when Tolkien found love and friendship as the inspiration for his artistic output.

    The film stars Nicholas Hoult as J.R. Tolkien and Lily Collins as Edith Bratt. The film also stars Derek Jacobi, Tom Glynn Carney, Anthony Boyle and Colm Meaney.

    Extremely Wicked, Shockingly Evil and Vile ($9.8 Million)

    Extremely Wicked, Shockingly Evil and Vile was released in theatres on May 3, 2019 and grossed about $9.8 million in the worldwide box office. The film was streamed through Netflix and was directed by Joe Berlinger. It is a biographical crime drama film about the life of the serial killer Ted Bundy. Hollywood A-lister Zac Efron plays as the lead role while Lily Collins plays as Liz Kendall.

    The film also stars Haley Joel Osment, Kaya Scodelario, John Malkovich and Big Bang Star Jim Parsons.

    Love, Rosie ($25 Million)

    Love, Rosie was released in February 3, 2015 and has grossed about $25 million in the worldwide box office. The film directed by Christian Ditter is about best friends Rosie and Alex played by Lily Collins and Sam Claflin. The pair has been inseperable since they were young however fate has a different plan for the two and they eventually took seperate paths.

    The film revolves around the pair's friendship and how it will survive the years and distance between them.

    Priest ($78.3 Million)

    Priest was released in May 13, 2011 and has grossed $78.3 million in the worldwide box office. The film directed by Scott Stewart is about the legendary warrior priest who had to fight off vampires during the centuries of war between humanity and vampires.

    The conflict arises after a pack of vampires abducted his niece and he decided to break his sacred vow and sets out on a mission to rescue her before the vampires bites her. He is joined on his quest by the boyfriend of her niece and a former priestess. Aside from Lily Collins, the film also stars Legion actor Paul Bettany, Karl Urban, Cam Gigandet, Maggie Q and Christopher Plummer.

    Abduction ($86.6 Million)

    Abduction was released in September 23, 2011 and has grossed $86.6 million in the worldwide box office. The film directed by John Singleton with the screenplay by Shawn Christensen is about Nathan Harper played by Taylor Lautner who has had an uneasy feeling about his life with his family. Soon, he found a picture of him as a youngster on a missing-persons website. He tries to uncover his true identity as he became a target of some assassins which led him to leave town with his neighbor Karen played by Lily Collins.

    Aside from Twilight actor Taylor Lautner and Lily Collins, the film also stars Alfred Molina, Sigourney Weaver, Oriah Acima Andrews, and Maria Bello.

    The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones ($95.3 million)

    The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones was released in August 21, 2013 and has grossed about $95.3 million in the worldwide box office. The film directed by Harald Zwart is about young girl named Clary Fray played by Lily Collins who leads a normal life. It was revealed to Clary that she descended from ancestors of Shadowhunters. Clary decided to join forces with the group Shadowhunters after he mother's disappearance. She entered the alternate world that is filled with monsters and evil called Downworld.

    The film also stars Jamie Campbell Bower, Kevin Zegers, Robert Sheehan, Hemima West and Lena Headey.

    Mirror Mirror ($183 Million)

    Mirror Mirror was released in March 30, 2012 and has grossed $183 million in the worldwide box office. The film is based on Snow White, and Grimms' Fairy Tales. It is directed by Tarsem Singh and is about an orphaned princess Snow White played by Lily Collins who is the rightful heir of her kingdom. Despite being the rightful heir to the throne, the evil jealous queen played by Julia Roberts has schemed a plan that eventually made her gain control of the kingdom. The film will revolve on how Snow White will regain the control of the kingdom.

    Aside from Julia Roberts and Lily Collins, the film also stars Armie Hammer, Mark Povinelli, Ronald Lee Clark, Sebastian Saraceno and Sean Bean.

    The Blind Side ($309 Million)

    The Blind Side was released on November 20, 2009 and has grossed $309 million on the worldwide box office. The film directed by John Lee Hancock is about a homeless black teen who went in and out of the school system for years. Leigh Anne Tuohy, played by Sandra Bullock, and her husband decided to take him in. The couple eventually became the legal guardians of the teen and transformed the life of everyone involved.

    Aside from America's sweetheart Sandra Bullock and Lily Collins, the film also stars Quinton Arron, Jae Head, Tim McGraw, and Kathy Bates.
