    No one is safe when Amanda Bynes starts trolling through these e-streets. In her latest stunt, the former Nickelodeon queen slammed the First Couple on Twitter. Barack Obama and Michelle Obama are ugly!

    04 Jul 2024, 00:00

    No one is safe when Amanda Bynes starts trolling through these e-streets. In her latest “stunt,” the former Nickelodeon queen slammed the First Couple on Twitter.

    Barack Obama and Michelle Obama are ugly!

    — Amanda Bynes (@AmandaBynes) July 8, 2013

    It could be e-retaliation for the direct plea for help she sent to President Obama after copping a DUI charge in April and being accused of a hit-and-run in May. “I don’t drink. Please fire the cop who arrested me. I also don’t hit and run. The end,” she told the Head of State. Still, “ugly” has been Bynes’ favorite adjective of choice, especially when throwing shots at celebs like Drake, Miley Cyrus and supermodel Chrissy Teigen. She even went after Twitter navy’s beloved Rihanna, who wasn’t scared to fire back. The “Amanda Show” star spewed, “@rihanna Chris Brown beat you because you’re not pretty enough … No one wants to be your lover so you call everyone and their mother.” RiRi then threw digital darts on her timeline without a specific mention, “Ya see what happens when they cancel Intervention?,” to which Bynes responded, “@rihanna Unlike your fugly faced self I don’t do drugs! U need the intervention dog! I met your ugly face in person. U aren’t pretty u know know!” May need a psychologist to handle this handle.
