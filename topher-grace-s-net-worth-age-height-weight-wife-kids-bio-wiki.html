    Early Life

    Christopher John “Topher” Grace was born on July 12, 1978, in New York City, New York, to his parents John Grace and Pat Grace. He was raised in Darien, Connecticut.

    Career

    In 1998, Topher Grace became a cast of the television show ‘That 70’s Show’. He appeared in the series till 2006. In 2005, he hosted an episode of ‘Saturday Night Live.’ He was seen in the TV film ‘Too Big to Fall’ in 2011. In 2012, he was seen in ‘The Beauty Inside.’

    He made his movie debut by appearing in the 2000 film ‘Traffic.’ In 2003, he was seen in the film ‘Mona Lisa Smile.’ He appeared as Pete Monash in ‘Win a Date with Tad Hamilton!’.
