    Isabelle Amarachi and Caleb Kelechi: Their Age and Bio

    Isabelle is the first child of her parents. She was born on 21 April 2014. As the first child of the couple, she brought immense happiness to her life. Considering Isabelle to be the answer to their prayer from god, they named her “Isabelle” which means “pledge to God” in French. Similarly, her middle name “Amarachi” also means “Grace of God”.

    Isabelle is currently 5 years old. And her younger brother Caleb is 3 years old.

    Two years after the birth of Isabelle, her parents gave birth to her younger brother Caleb Kelechi Asomugha on October 5, 2016.
