    Steve Harvey is a father to seven children (four of them are daughters), so he is familiar with the dating process and has met many potential partners over the years. He may appear to be one of the nicest guys in showbiz, but when it comes to his daughters, Harvey doesn't want anyone who will not treat them well, so he can be challenging to win over.

    "If I spot a bad one, I get rid of them," Harvey told Us Weekly in an interview in March 2024. "I make no bones about it, so they're very careful when they bring them."

    What Has Steve Harvey Said About The Men His Daughters Date?

    In the interview with Us Weekly, Harvey spoke about how people try to give a good impression, or, as he refers to it, they have a mask. "You can think a person is wonderful, but everybody's wonderful in the beginning. Everybody, you always meet the mask," he said. "The mask can last for about 90 days, and then after a while, the mask starts unraveling, and I just try to wait on that period. Sometimes they make it, sometimes they don't."

    "But if you come in the house, and I know right off the top that you're just not a good guy, I start the reading process right away," he shared. "I don't say nothing to my daughter, I just, behind the scenes, I start working toward getting rid of this guy." His approach to his daughters' dating indicates how much Harvey cares about who they associate with, and he is a protective dad. He may not be the easiest guy to impress, but his actions do come from love.

    "The person you choose to spend your life with is probably the most important decision you'll ever make," he said. "That relationship becomes an elevator. It can take you up, or it can take you down, so choose wisely." Harvey knows what he is talking about, and while his first marriage to Marcia Harvey did not work out, he and Marjorie Harvey have been married since 2007.

    What Has Lori Harvey Said About Her Dad Approving Her Partners?

    Steve Harvey's daughter Lori Harvey has had some high-profile romances, including Damson Idris (Idris Elba's son), Michael B. Jordan, and Future. When she split from Jordan in June 2022, everyone wanted to know what went wrong, and her dad was asked about it in an interview on Today with Hoda and Jenna. He said he would respect his daughter's privacy, refusing to talk about her personal life. But he did praise her, saying, "I think she's in a really good place in her life right now. I think she's content with Lori and I think that's a great place for her to start from."
