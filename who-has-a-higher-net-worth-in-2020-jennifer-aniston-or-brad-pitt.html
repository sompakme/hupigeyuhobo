    Who Has A Higher Net Worth In 2020: Jennifer Aniston Or Brad Pitt?

    Brad Pitt and Jennifer Aniston are two of the most successful actors. Together, they also represent one of the most famous ex-couples in Hollywood history. Interestingly, both also have an incredible net worth. Now, its just a matter of figuring out whos worth more today.

    01 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Brad Pitt and Jennifer Aniston are two of the most successful actors. Together, they also represent one of the most famous ex-couples in Hollywood history. Interestingly, both also have an incredible net worth. Now, it’s just a matter of figuring out who’s worth more today.

    Everything Changed For Jennifer Aniston After Being Cast In Friends

    In the beginning, it seemed like that Aniston wasn’t having much luck with her Hollywood career. “We cast her in a few more pilots, but none were very good,” Warren Littlefield, the former president of NBC Entertainment, told Vanity Fair. “One night while gassing up my car on Sunset Boulevard in Hollywood, I ran into Jennifer, and she asked me, ‘Will it ever happen for me?’ God, I wanted it to.”

    As you know, it did happen for Aniston in the form of the NBC sitcom Friends. The moment she was cast to play the role of Rachel Green, everyone suddenly knew who Aniston was. More importantly, her performance earned her five Emmy nominations and one Emmy win. Meanwhile, the success of the show also meant that Aniston and her co-stars got paid more and more each season. Towards the end of the show’s run, the Friends cast even received a hefty $1 million per episode.

    Related: Here’s How Paul Rudd Almost Ran Jennifer Aniston Over

    Aside from being paid lots of money for Friends, Aniston also managed to secure a lot of endorsement deals. These involved brands such as Glaceau Smartwater, Aveeno, and Emirates. It is believed that endorsements alone result in a $10 million payout for Aniston annually, according to Fox Business.

    RELATED: Friends: How Much Did Jennifer Aniston Get Paid For The Show?

    Since Friends, Aniston has also ventured out into films. Over the years, she’s starred in movies such as Along Came Polly, Rumor Has It…, Marley & Me, Horrible Bosses, Wanderlust, Cake, Mother’s Day, Dumplin’, and many others. In addition, Aniston also starred opposite good friend Adam Sandler in Netflix’s Murder Mystery. At the same time, Aniston decided to make a return to television in 2019 with the Apple TV+ series The Morning Show. According to Business Insider, Aniston is estimated to be worth $200 million.

    Brad Pitt Started In Commercials Then Became A Movie Star

    He started out as a guy in a Pringles commercial. Since then, Pitt has gone on to star in several hit movies. In the 90s, he starred in Interview with the Vampire: The Vampire Chronicles, Legends of the Fall, Se7en, 12 Monkeys, The Devil’s Own, Seven Years in Tibet, and Fight Club.

    He also followed this up with films like Ocean’s Eleven, Confessions of a Dangerous Mind, Troy, Mr. & Mrs. Smith, Babel, The Curious Case of Benjamin Button, Inglorious Basterds, Moneyball, World War Z, Fury and 12 Years a Slave. More recently, Pitt also starred alongside Leonardo DiCaprio in the critical hit Once Upon a Time…in Hollywood. Interestingly, Pitt has been known to take a pay cut from time to time. However, he often negotiates to get backend points in exchange.

    Just like Aniston, Pitt has also been known to have several lucrative endorsement deals. For instance, Pitt became the first male actor to take part in a campaign for Chanel No. 5 fragrance. According to a report from GoBankingRates.com that was published by Yahoo, this resulted in a $7 million payout.

    RELATED: Why Brad Pitt Is Always Eating In His Movies

    Meanwhile, Pitt is also one of the few actors who are actively working behind the scenes thanks to his own production company, Plan B Entertainment. Pitt co-founded the production company with Aniston, but he became the company’s sole owner following the couple’s divorce. In case you didn’t know, Plan B has been involved in the production of films such as The Departed, Moonlight, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory and Eat, Pray, Love. And because of his producer credits, you can be sure that Pitt is getting a share of the films’ box office earnings. According to a report from Men’s Health, Pitt is estimated to be worth a cool $300 million today.

    An Insight Into Brad Pitt and Jennifer Aniston’s Future Earnings

    Despite the ongoing situation right now, you can be sure that both Pitt and Aniston are still poised to earn millions. In the case of Aniston, you can expect to see her star in the second season of The Morning Show alongside fellow A-lister Reese Witherspoon. It also just so happens that Aniston’s company, Echo Films, is co-producing the show, so she gets a cut from the series’ earnings. In addition, Aniston is also said to be an executive producer in the highly anticipated Friends reunion.

    As for Pitt, the actor is said to be starring in the upcoming film Bullet Train alongside The Kissing Booth’s Joey King. Aside from this, Pitt will also star opposite actress Emma Stone in the upcoming film Babylon. In addition, IMDb records also reveal that Pitt is serving as a producer on several projects slated for 2021. Clearly, both Pitt and Aniston are set to make millions of dollars in the coming months and years. And at some point, it might even be possible for the exes to achieve equal net worth.
