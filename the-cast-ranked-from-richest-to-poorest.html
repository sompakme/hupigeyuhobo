    The Conjuring Universe is a successful and interconnected series of horror films created by director James Wan, inspired by the very real and very scary paranormal investigations of Ed and Lorraine Warren. The series is well-known for its terrifying supernatural tales and well-known characters, which are all the more unsettling because a lot of them actually happened. Several of the Conjuring Universe stars have appeared in more than one of the horror movies, with each Conjuring Universe movie boosting the actors' net worth.

    Related:The Truth About 'The Conjuring' Investigators, Ed And Lorraine Warren

    The franchise includes The Conjuring, which follows the Warrens' cases, as well as spin-off films like Annabelle, The Nun, and The Curse of La Llorona, each exploring paranormal tales and entities. There is a reason the Conjuring Universe films have earned over $2 billion at the box office: they are terrifying. The successful films feature reoccurring characters, but who is the richest Conjuring Universe cast member?

    12 Vera Farmiga

    Net Worth: $10 Million

    Character:

    Lorraine Warren

    Conjuring Universe Movies Starred In:

    The Conjuring (2013), The Conjuring 2 (2016), The Nun (2018), Annabelle Comes Home (2019), The Conjuring: The Devil Made Me Do It (2021), The Nun 2 (2023)

    Where To Watch:

    The Conjuring on Hulu

    In The Conjuring Universe, Vera Farmiga assumed the role of Lorraine Warren (1927–2019), a real-life paranormal investigator. Her portrayal of Lorraine Warren is central to the franchise, and she has appeared in almost all 8 movies within this chilling cinematic universe.

    However, Vera Farmiga's contribution to the world of cinema goes well beyond her involvement in The Conjuring Universe. She has delivered memorable performances in a range of other films that have done very well at the box office. Some of these notable films include The Departed, a Martin Scorsese film; Orphan, a psychological horror film; and Up in the Air, a dramedy. However, while these films did well, they are not her top-producing films.

    Vera Farmiga's top three box office hits, according to The Numbers, are:

    • Godzilla, King of the Monsters: $383 million
    • The Conjuring: $316 million
    • The Conjuring 2: $310 million

    And Farmiga's talents aren't limited to just the big screen. She has also made a significant impact on television. One of her standout roles was in the popular TV series Bates Motel, a contemporary prequel to Alfred Hitchcock's iconic film Psycho. In the series, Farmiga took on the challenging role of Norma Bates, mother to the complex and disturbed character Norman Bates.

    In terms of wealth, Vera Farmiga's net worth is currently estimated at $10 million. That number is likely to see further growth in the near future, though. She is set to reprise her role as Lorraine Warren in two upcoming movies from The Conjuring Universe: The Conjuring 4: Last Rites and Annabelle 4.

    11 Linda Cardellini

    Net Worth: $9 Million

    Character:

    Anna Tate-Garcia

    Conjuring Universe Movie Starred In:

    The Curse of La Llorona (2019)

    Where To Watch:

    The Curse of La Llorona on Max

    Linda Cardellini played the lead role of Anna Tate-Garcia in the 2019 horror film The Curse of La Llorona. The movie revolves around a social worker and her family who become entangled in the supernatural horrors of La Llorona, a malicious ghost from Mexican folklore who is known for her relentless pursuit of children.

    One of her breakthrough roles was on the popular TV series Freaks and Geeks, where she portrayed the character Lindsay Weir. Linda Cardellini's net worth further increased by appearing in movies like Scooby-Doo (as Velma), Legally Blonde (as Chutney Windham), and Brokeback Mountain (as Cassie Cartwright). However, her performance as Lylla in Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 is her most recent and made more at the box office then all three of those movies combined. Coming in at $845 million, the Marvel Universe films are hard to beat financially.

    Linda Cardellini was also recently in the Netflix hit series Dead to Me. She played the lead role of Judy Hale, opposite Christina Applegate. While there are no financials on what she was paid for this series, Variety reports that Applegate made $325,000 per episode in the third and final season of Dead to Me. Cardellini likely made a similar figure seeing her role was vital to shows success.
