In this article, we'll take a look at what fans said about the lyrics on "Midnights." We'll also look at why "Midnights" has done so well and what Taylor Swift thinks about the album.

    Fans Criticized Some Of Taylor Swift's 'Midnights' Lyrics

    While there's no doubt that fans love Taylor Swift's "Midnights" album, many couldn't help but share that they find some of the lyrics to be silly and awkward. Several fans discussed "Midnights" in the Taylor Swift SubReddit and admitted that there are some lines that they just don't like.

    The song "Vigilante Sh*t" was mentioned a few times in the thread. Redditor @SenorWoodley said:

    "'Draw the cat eye sharp enough to kill a man.' Cringe cringe cringe. I’m sorry."

    Redditor @icefrozen ground mentioned the lyrics "I don't start sh*t but I can tell you how it ends." They wrote, "It sounds like something I would have said as an edgy fourteen year old." Redditor @hdouglas447 added, "Big AIM away message energy."

    A few fans shared that they don't love "Snow On The Beach." Since Lana Del Rey sings on the song, it's one of the tunes that got the most hype when "Midnights" was released.

    Inside The Success Of Taylor Swift's "Midnights" Album

    The success of Taylor Swift's "Midnights" album continues to be incredibly impressive. In January 2023, The Guardian reported that Universal Music made $230 million from the album. According to Entertainment Weekly, Billboard calculated that "Midnights" had 357 million streams the first week that it was released, and 3 million sales around the world.

    Thanks to the love that fans had for this album immediately, "Midnights" took the number one spot on the Billboard 200 right away. 10 of Swift's "Midnights" songs also made it into the top 10 of the Billboard Hot 100, which no other singer had been able to achieve before.

    According to NBC, Swift now has 11 albums that made the number one spot on the Billboard 20. By October 31st, only a few days after the album came out, "Midnights" had sold the most copies of any 2022 album.
