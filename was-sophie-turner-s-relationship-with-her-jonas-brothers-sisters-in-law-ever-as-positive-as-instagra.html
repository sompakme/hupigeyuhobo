    Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas met via social media, and before Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas started their relationship, Joe messaged her on Instagram. It makes sense that two of the Jonas Brothers started their marriages this way since they seem very comfortable sharing their lives online. Music superstars Joe, Nick, and Kevin Jonas and their three wives all have millions of followers and like to post about personal moments as well as career accomplishments.

    Now that Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas are getting divorced, all eyes are on her relationship with her sisters-in-law Danielle Jonas and Priyanka Chopra. Based on the Instagram posts that fans have seen, it seems like the three stars truly and genuinely love each other. Here's whether they really got along...

    What Did Sophie Turner, Priyanka Chopra, And Danielle Jonas Share On Instagram About Their Relationship?

    Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas have two daughters, and before their divorce announcement, things seemed to be pretty much perfect between them. The couple got married in 2019 and had weddings in Provence and Las Vegas.

    Ever since she began her relationship with Joe Jonas, Sophie Turner has posted Instagram photos of herself and her sisters-in-law. She has shared heartwarming photos of big family dinners. She has also celebrated the Jonas Brothers's achievements with her new family. These posts definitely make it seem like they all get along great and love being together.

    In January 2023, the Jonas Brothers received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. Sophie Turner shared a photo on Instagram of herself, Joe Jonas, Nick Jonas and Priyanka Chopra, and Kevin Jonas and Danielle Jonas along with their two daughters Valentina and Alena. Everyone looked happy to be there.

    Sophie wrote a nice message in her Instagram caption about the Jonas Brothers. She said, "To see the hard work and love and light you boys bring to your music… it’s an honor just to watch from the sidelines. After almost 20 years of hard work you boys are so deserving of this. I’m so lucky to count you as family. I’m proud of you every day."

    Priyanka Chopra also posted a photo with Sophie Turner after her Bachelorette in November 2018. The sisters-in-law were in Amsterdam along with some of Priyanka's friends. Priyanka wrote "The morning after" in the caption of the photo and added the hashtag #JSisters. People reported that Sophie also shared a picture of Priyanka wearing a tiara on her own social media account.

    Danielle Jonas didn't post any photos with Sophie Turner on her Instagram in 2023 or 2022. She seems to mostly post about her husband Kevin Jonas and their daughters, though.

    Inside Sophie Turner's Relationship With Priyanka Chopra And Danielle Jonas

    Everyone feels some sort of pressure to post pretty and positive things on Instagram. While sometimes sharing the raw and hard parts of life feels appropriate, most people would agree that they don't always want to talk about that stuff online. Celebrities must feel even more pressure to make it seem as if their lives are perfect.

    Sophie Turner has always seemed to love her two sisters-in-law, and she has never said negative things about them. However, Danielle Jonas was honest about Sophie Turner and Priyanka Chopra and explained that she sometimes has a hard time.

    According to People, Danielle shared some vulnerable thoughts on the LadyGang podcast. She explained, “I am so different than him. It’s actually made it work even more because we’re not trying to fight for the fight celebrity part of it or whatever, but it is a love-hate. I feel like I am torn.”

    Danielle continued in her podcast interview, “The two boys married somebody who, they’re actresses, they’re out there, everyone knows them. And I feel like I’m Danielle, and it’s hard. It’s also that I’m out there celebrity-wise because I’ve married you."

    It's interesting to hear Danielle's perspective on marrying into a famous family and also having famous sisters-in-law. People can definitely understand how she feels and anyone in the same situation would likely say the exact same thing.

    According to Hello! Magazine, after Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas got married, Sophie shared how much she was enjoying being married. Sophie Turner also discussed her two sisters-in-law in the same interview.

    Sophie said, "It's also nice to have built-in girlfriends who are actually really cool, who I can hang out with and we can really talk to each other about, like, how crazy the boys' lives are. We can relate on so many different levels. It's like, thank God, because you never know [about your in-laws]."

    It sounds like Sophie genuinely loves Priyanka and Danielle. There have never been any reports of the three fighting or having a hard time with each other. It sounds like they embraced each other as soon as they met.

    However, now that Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas are splitting up, it's possible that Sophie won't see Priyanka Chopra or Danielle Jonas as often as she did before. Fans can assume that since the three love hanging out together, they will do their best to keep spending time with each other.

    But there's no denying the fact that it will feel different. While of course Sophie might still be invited to family gatherings since she and Joe will be co-parenting their two children, no one can be sure what that will look like.

    In early September 2023, People reported that Joe Jonas had hired a divorce lawyer. A source then told the publication, "They were not separated but they've been living separate lives for months."
