    Westworld creators Lisa Joy and Jonathan Nolan announced their collaboration with Prime Video in 2018 with plans to adapt William Gibson’s novel into a series. Named The Peripheral after the book's title, the show received a series order a year later when casting began. Chloë Grace Moretz and Gary Carr were the first cast members announced as lead actors of the series in 2020 before the rest were detailed a year later. The show's filming began after the pandemic restrictions eased in London in mid-2021. The science-fiction theme of the series revolves around a near-future of Earth where technology has changed society and is morphing into a new future.

    The unique idea of the eight-episode first season was released in 2022 on Prime Video and was met with positive reviews. Along with the storyline, the show's cast was praised for their performance brought to life. Let’s look at The Peripheral's cast and their net worth.

    Jack Reynor ($2 million)

    Jack Reynor portrays the role of Burton Fisher in The Peripheral, who is a caring son and brother. He is the brother of Moretz’s character and a protective mercenary for everyone. Reynor first came to prominence after starring in Transformers: Age Of Extinction and went on to win a special award at the Sundance Film Festival for his performance in Glassland. He has also starred in the critically-acclaimed film Midsommar.

    Gary Carr ($3 million)

    After studying at a music theater and graduating from drama school in London, Gary Carr began his career in theater. He worked in several plays before landing a lead role in Death In Paradise, a BBC series. Carr is also known for his performance in the fourth season of Downton Abbey. He portrays the character of Wilf Netherton, who is helping the protagonist find a way to channel their power, as noted by Screen Rant.

    T'Nia Miller ($3 million)

    Dr. Cherise Nuland is the main antagonist of the series and the head of the evil Research Institute, and T’Nia Miller plays the character to perfection. The actress has worked in many BBC TV productions and starred in two Netflix series: Sex Education and The Haunting Of Bly Manor.

    Louis Herthum ($5 million)

    After working with the creators on Westworld, Louis Herthum reunited with them to star in The Peripheral. He began working in local theater in the 1980s before starring in print ads in the 1990s. Herthum has appeared in shows such as Breaking Bad, True Detective, and The Mentalist. He stars in The Peripheral as a ruthless negative character who plays dirty.

    Katie Leung ($5 million)

    A gutsy and fearless character, Katie Leung is a technical professional in The Peripheral named Ash. Her character overlooks manipulating technology and turns against the organization to save people. As noted by Variety, Leung is best known for starring as Cho Cheng in several Harry Potter films. Her other acclaimed performances include Annika, The Foreigner, and Strangers.

    Alexandra Billings ($5 million)

    Alexandra Billings is a veteran actress who has appeared in popular shows such as How To Get Away With Murder, Grey’s Anatomy, and ER. In 2005, she became the second openly transgender woman to star in a movie with her performance in Romy and Michele: In the Beginning. In the series, she stars as Inspector Ainsley Lowbeer.

    JJ Feild ($7 million)

    Since the series first introduced the main characters, it took one year before the supporting cast was announced. As mentioned by Deadline, there was high anticipation from fans when JJ Feild was to take over the role of the antagonist Lev. Lev is a member of a mysterious and influential group in London called Klept. Some of Feild’s best projects include Austenland and Captain America: The First Avenger.

    Charlotte Riley ($8 million)

    Portraying the complex role of Aelita West, Charlotte Riley has a layered character to play in The Peripheral. She became prominent after starring in ITV’s production of Wuthering Heights in 2009. The actress also earned notoriety for her supporting role in the hit British drama Peaky Blinders. Riley’s other credits include Edge Of Tomorrow, The Cherry Orchard, and Easy Virtue.

    Eli Goree ($11 million)

    Eli Goree’s career has recently taken off after his recurring roles in the HBO series Ballers and CW’s Riverdale. He is known for his sci-fi projects, including The 100 and Eve Of Destruction. In The Peripheral, Goree plays a member of the Marines–Conner Penske, helping the protagonists fight against the evil forces.

    Chloë Grace Moretz ($12 million)

    Chloë Grace Moretz started her acting career as a child when she appeared in drama films and television shows in the early 2010s. She made a breakthrough with her 2010 lead role in Kick-Ass and began starring in various projects. Moretz became the leading lady in The Peripheral, taking on the role of Flynne Fisher, as stated by Collider. Her character is solid and confident, showcasing fearlessly in times of crisis.

    Other notable cast members include Melinda Page Hamilton, Alex Hernandez, Adelind Horan, Austin Rising, and David Hoflin. The Peripheral has caught audiences' attention with its enrapturing plot that keeps everyone on their toes, and the show's success could lead to its renewal for a second season.
