    Coachella keeps getting exciting every year. The festival is known for attracting many celebrities and influencers, who often attend the event with their friends or partners. Coachella’s atmosphere and the presence of many high-profile individuals contribute further to the high spirits and intrigue among fans and the media.

    RELATED: Rolling Loud Miami And Coachella Paid These Musicians An Absolute Fortune To Headline Their Festival

    Dating rumors between celebrities who attend Coachella together are quite common. In some cases, these rumors may be true, however, in many cases, the rumors are simply speculation, and the celebrities may just be friends or acquaintances. Former flame, Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello are not the first ones to ignite dating rumors, when they decided to attend Coachella together after calling it quits for two years.

    Although, according to Page Six, it appears Mendes and Cabello are very much back together after their Coachella fling. The same can't necessarily be said for other A-list attendees...

    8 Shawn Mendes And Camila Cabello Are Dating After Making Out At Coachella

    The internet was sent into a frenzy after a video of Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello sharing a passionate kiss during the first week of Coachella went viral. The clip, which was shared by the Twitter account Pop Base, shows the two hitmakers embracing before locking lips. Fans speculate if this could mean the former couple is back together since their amicable split in November 2021.

    Intially, sources close to Mendes and Cabello told Entertainment Tonight that both "knew they were both heading to Coachella and had plans to meet up and hang out" but are not back together. The unnamed source also claimed that the former couple has been in contact in recent weeks and has had conversations about their breakup.

    "There is still a connection between them, and both of them shared they still care about one another. It's very casual at this point."

    However, more recent sources, including Page Six and People have stated that the two are very much back together, proving that not all Coachella dates are purely rumors.

    RELATED: Travis Scott Desperately Wanted To Play Coachella 2022, Reportedly Offered To Perform For Free

    Prior to this, Mendes was linked to Sabrina Carpenter, after being spotted together in Los Angeles in February. Meanwhile, Cabello recently ended her relationship with Lox Club CEO Austin Kevitch, whom she dated for less than a year.

    7 Leonardo DiCaprio And Irina Shayk Were Seen Partying At Coachella Together

    Hollywood heartthrob Leonardo DiCaprio is back in the dating spotlight after being spotted with Russian model Irina Shayk, who happens to be the ex-girlfriend of Bradley Cooper. The two were reportedly seen having a blast with a group of friends at the Neon Carnival, hosted by Levi's and Tequila Don Julio, at the Desert International Horse Park in Thermal, California.

    According to TMZ, DiCaprio tried to keep a low profile during the night out, sporting a black baseball cap and a mask to cover his face. Meanwhile, the stunning 37-year-old model made a fashion statement with her eye-catching floral ensemble, which included a crop top and a long skirt.

    However, a source told ET, "Leo and Irina are not dating… They hung out in separate cabanas and not with each other. Irina was with her publicist and friends and Leo was with friends and some girls. Both of them had a great time doing their own thing."

    6 Kendall Jenner And Bad Bunny Intensified Their Dating Rumors During Coachella

    Kendall Jenner was spotted watching Bad Bunny headline the first weekend of Coachella. The model was seen dancing and smiling in the crowd during the rapper's set, as captured by a fan's TikTok video.

    Kendall and Bad Bunny first set tongues wagging with romance rumors in February, just four months after her split with Phoenix Suns player, Devin Booker. Even if fans think that dating Bad Bunny was Kendall's worst move, the two were seen leaving Beyoncé and Jay-Z's Oscars 2023 after-party together.

    They were also spotted on a romantic horseback riding date at a ranch in California. The rumored couple hit the trail together, with the rapper sitting behind Kendall as they enjoyed the picturesque surroundings.

    5 Ariana Madix Confirmed Her New Relationship With Daniel Wai During Coachella

    Ariana Madix appeared to have moved on from her past, with her cheating ex-boyfriend Tom Sandoval, after nine years of being together. On the second day of the Coachella music festival, the 37-year-old reality star was seen all over her new beau, Daniel Wai.

    As per TMZ, the alleged new couple was spotted having a great time on Day 2, and they were filmed kissing during one of the final sets of the evening. Footage captured the reality TV star and her new partner sharing a passionate kiss, not just once, but at least twice. It appears that the couple was thoroughly enjoying each other's company at the festival.

    Although the two have not confirmed their relationship, Daniel Wai took to his Instagram story to share a photo of Madix and him getting close and cozy together. The reality star later re-shared the photo on her Instagram account, sparking fan speculation about the status of their romance.

    4 Lili Reinhart Sparked Dating Rumors With Spencer Neville After Coachella

    Lili Reinhart kept her personal life low-key since his public split with Cole Sprouse in 2020, but sparked dating rumors in 2022. The former Riverdale actress was spotted at the Neon Carnival during Coachella 2022, holding hands with actor Spencer Neville.

    In the photos shared by Just Jared, Reinhart and Neville were seen leaving the festival together with their hands tightly interlocked. While the pair never confirmed nor denied their relationship, rumors about their dating stopped after they were not seen in public together after Coachella.

    More recently, Lili Reinhart has been romantically linked to Jack Martin of Tik Tok fame.

    3 Tyga And Iggy Azalea Were Rumored To Be Dating After Being Spotted At Coachella

    Before Tyga and Avril Lavigne officially started dating, rumors swirled around Tyga and Iggy Azalea since the two appeared together on Valentine’s Day-themed episode of TRL. But it was their Coachella weekend in 2018 that got fans talking. The two rappers were spotted together at the TAO x Revolve Desert Nights party, where they reportedly spent hours talking in a private backstage area.

    According to E! News, Tyga posted a photo on his first night of Coachella 2018, that featured a pair of women’s Vans shoes, a Louis Vuitton bag, and another designer bag. Later that night, Azalea posted an Instagram photo showing a similar pair of Vans shoes. Though neither has confirmed anything, fans were convinced at that time, that the two were more than just friends.

    2 Did Leonardo DiCaprio And Rihanna Ever Date?

    In a star-studded event during the Coachella music festival back in 2016, Leonardo DiCaprio and Rihanna were spotted enjoying themselves at the Neon Carnival party. The two were photographed hanging out together, but a source told People that their interaction was purely platonic.

    "Leo slipped into the party incognito and was dancing and singing to 'No Scrubs,'... a couple of tables over, Rihanna was dancing with a couple of girlfriends. Leo saw her and made his way over to say hi."

    1 Sean Penn And Sienna Miller Were Rumored To Be Dating After Being Seen Partying At Coachella

    Hollywood stars Sean Penn and Sienna Miller were spotted together at the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival in 2008. The two were seen posing for a photo, sparking rumors about their relationship status. However, Miller clarified that they were just old friends, despite Penn appearing quite pleased to be in her company.
