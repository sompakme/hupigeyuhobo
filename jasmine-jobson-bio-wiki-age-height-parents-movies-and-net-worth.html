    Jasmine Jobson Career

    Jobson began her acting career in theatre, performing in plays such as Phoenix: A Girl on Fire and Wild Diamonds at the Hackney Down Studios in 2013.

    From 2014, Jobson performed in several short films Flea, A Generation of Vipers, and The King, before debuting on television in 2016 as Kia Hopkins in a single episode of Suspects (TV series). 

    Since then she has made minor appearances in The Break (TV series), Five by Five, and Dark Heart (TV series) as Sally Watkins, before landing a main character role in the 2019 Netflix British television crime drama series Top Boy. 

    In 2020, Jobson stars as Lily in the feature film Surge, which premiered in January 2020 at the Sundance Film Festival in Salt Lake City.  Jobson purported to be comfortable in an airport environment, the setting for Surge, as she once worked as a bartender in Wetherspoons at Heathrow’s Terminal 2.
